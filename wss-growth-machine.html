🚀 Outreach & Self-Optimization Pipeline

5 workflows extending the lead engine with draft-first outreach, thread-based reply detection, step-tracked follow-up sequencing, newsletter-to-prospect bridging, and weekly self-optimization.

Workflow Schedule Function Status
WF5 Outreach Sender Every 12h Creates Gmail drafts + logs draft_id (no auto-send). Review in Gmail, WF6 tracks replies once sent. READY
WF6 Reply Detection Every 4h Thread-based reply detection via Gmail thread IDs. Only tracks sent emails (not drafts). READY
WF7 Weekly Self-Optimization Mon 9am Analyses metrics, generates recommendations, tracks source quality READY
WF8 Newsletter Bridge Daily Scores MailerLite engagement, matches business email domains to prospects, flags nurture candidates. READY
WF9 Follow-up Sequencer Daily Day 0→3→7→10→14 follow-ups. Notifies Slack + advances sequence_step (fires once per step). READY
🧠 Self-Improving Database
5 new Supabase tables track source quality, A/B test variants, prompt performance, and weekly system metrics. The machine learns which RSS feeds and outreach angles produce results.
📬 Draft-First Outreach
WF5 creates Gmail drafts and stores draft_id + thread_id. Status stays 'draft_created' until you manually send. WF6 only starts reply tracking once gmail_thread_id exists (i.e. after send).
🔄 Step-Tracked Follow-ups
WF9 checks last_notified_step so each follow-up fires exactly once: Day 3 email, Day 7 LinkedIn, Day 10 email, Day 14 break-up. Advances sequence_step after each Slack notification.
